Use Meet on any device and from anywhere

Guests can join meetings and calls from their computer, using any modern web browser. They do not need to install any other software. On mobile devices, they can join meetings and calls from the Google Meet app. You can also join meetings and calls using Google Nest Hub Max.

Meet is for everyone

Google Meet makes meetings even easier with Live Captions powered by Google's speech recognition technology. Live captions make it easier to understand what's happening in a meeting with foreign language speakers and people with hearing loss. Plus, it's easy to follow a meeting in any noisy location with Live Caption (available in English only).

stay connected to the meeting

With easy schedule creation, easy recording, and customizable layouts, people stay engaged.

share your screen

Present your documents, slides, and spreadsheets by showing your entire screen or just one window.

hosting a large meeting

You can invite people from the same domain or outside of that domain to the meeting. Their number should not exceed 500.

Join a meeting from your phone

Use the Google Meet app to join a video call, or join an audio-only call by calling the dialing number in the meeting call.

making decisions about meetings

These meetings are secure by default. The host of the meeting can decide who will attend the meeting. Only people who have received permission to join the meeting from the host can participate in it.

broadcasting internal events

You can live stream events like town halls and sales meetings to up to 100,000 people in your domain
